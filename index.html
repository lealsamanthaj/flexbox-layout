Hi, I'm Samantha Leal

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and providing the best user experience. When I'm not working, or pursuing side projects, you can find me at my favorite bookstore or out on the trail taking amateur photos of Central Texas flora and views of the Hill Country.

I was born and raised in Austin, Texas, but my roots reach to South America. I love to travel and photograph different regions of the United States. We never had money for vacations as a child so I jump on every opportnity to explore the world.
My passion is art and the abundance of unique work you can explore through different mediums. I love beautiful things and capturing them with my camera is what makes me the happiest. The outdoors inspire the color schemes and layouts I choose.
I originally went to school to study economics and human behavior. Somewhere along the way, I found a happy middle-ground that allows me to understand the market for art and how it reflects the time, effort, and authenticity of an artist work.
